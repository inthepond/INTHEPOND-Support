BaMai Privacy Policy

Overview

BaMai is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data while using our app.

Data Collection

We collect the following data to provide our services:

  • Pulse readings from Apple Watch sensors
  • Basic health metrics for tracking purposes
  • User preferences and settings
  • App usage statistics for improvement

Data Storage and Security

All personal health data is:

  • Stored securely on your device
  • Protected using industry-standard encryption
  • Never shared without your explicit consent
  • Backed up securely through iCloud (if enabled)

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing pulse tracking and analysis
  • Generating personal health insights
  • Improving app functionality
  • Anonymous statistical analysis

Third-Party Services

We integrate with Apple HealthKit to provide comprehensive health tracking. Any data sharing with HealthKit follows Apple's privacy guidelines and requires your explicit permission.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from our services
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Request data export

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our services evolve. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
