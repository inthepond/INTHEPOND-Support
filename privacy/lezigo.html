Lezigo Privacy Policy

Overview

Lezigo is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data while using our app.

Data Collection

We collect the following data to provide our services:

  • Account information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Event participation and hosting data
  • Location data (when actively using the app)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Photos and media shared within the community

Data Storage and Security

All personal data is:

  • Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Protected in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Regularly backed up to prevent data loss
  • Subject to strict access controls and monitoring

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing cultural experience matching and recommendations
  • Facilitating community connections and event organizing
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Personalizing cultural content to your interests

Community Content

When you share content in the Lezigo community:

  • You retain ownership of your content
  • You grant Lezigo license to display and distribute your content within the platform
  • You can delete your content at any time
  • Community guidelines are enforced to ensure respectful interactions

Third-Party Services

We may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These integrations follow our privacy standards, and any data sharing requires your explicit consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Export your data in a portable format

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our services evolve. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
