2. 应用程序性质 | Nature of the Application

BaMai是一款基于传统中医理论的脉象模拟和健康追踪应用程序。本应用：

BaMai is a pulse simulation and health tracking application based on Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) theory. This application:

  • 提供脉象模拟功能，帮助用户了解中医脉诊基础知识 | Provides pulse simulation features to help users learn basic TCM pulse diagnosis knowledge

3. 健康建议来源与限制 | Sources and Limitations of Health Advice

本应用提供的健康建议主要基于以下传统中医典籍：

Health advice provided by this application is primarily based on the following traditional TCM texts:

  • 《黄帝内经》(Yellow Emperor's Classic of Internal Medicine)
  • 《脉经》(The Pulse Classic)
  • 《濒湖脉学》(Binhu's Studies on Pulse)
  • 其他经典中医文献 | Other classical TCM literature

重要提醒：这些建议仅供参考和学习，不构成医疗建议。任何健康问题请咨询合格的医疗专业人员。
